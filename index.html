NoToHunger is a project built around SDG Goal 2, which is: Zero hunger. 
It aims to end all forms of malnutrition, including achieving, by 2025, the internationally agreed targets on stunting and wasting in children under 5 years of age, and address the nutritional needs of adolescent girls, pregnant and lactating women, and older persons.

  • Awareness and providing relevant expertise to enhance the agricultural ecosystem.
  • Reduce food wastage.
  • Food availability for all people.
  • Educate farmers about the importance of irrigation.
  • Creation of online forms to source donations to affected areas.

Our Vision is to, Create a world free of hunger where everyone gets a nutritious plate of food at least three times a day daily.

No to Hunger (NTH) is committed to ensuring that nutritious food is available to people all over the world by creating social safety nets, access to markets for rural farmers, resilience to climate change and end cycle of conflict and hunger; eliminating malnutrition, food wastage and loss.​
